Privacy Policy — Idukki Rush

Last updated: June 7, 2026

Idukki Rush is a free mobile game developed by Roshan Oommen Reji. This page explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Information We Collect

Idukki Rush does not directly collect any personal information. However, the app uses third-party services that may collect information:

Advertising (Unity Ads)

This app uses Unity Ads to display rewarded video ads. Unity Ads may collect and use data including device identifiers, advertising ID, IP address, and usage data to serve personalised or non-personalised ads.

For more information on how Unity uses data, please visit: https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

You can opt out of personalised advertising by adjusting your device's advertising settings (Settings → Privacy → Ads).

Data Storage

The app stores your game progress (high score, coins, upgrade levels) locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to any server.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
